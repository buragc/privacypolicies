# Privacy Policy for Similars Cleaner

**Effective Date:** 09/06/2025

Similars Cleaner values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Data Collection

- **No personal information is collected, stored, or shared.**
- The app scans photos only on your device to identify duplicates.
- **No data is transmitted, uploaded, or shared** to any external servers or third parties.

## Data Usage

- **All processing is done locally** on your device.
- Photos and related data never leave your device.
- **You remain in full control** of your information at all times.

## Third Parties

- Similars Cleaner does **not** use third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.
- Since no data leaves your device, no information is shared with third parties.

## Security

- No data is transmitted externally, so risks related to data transfer are eliminated.
- Your photos and information remain securely stored **only on your device**.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated policy will be published on this page.
